About

PingPong is a web app for managing a rankings ladder.

PingPong was made to keep track of results in casual competition leagues and give objective rankings of skill.

Instructions

To get started make a league, make some teams and add them to your league, and you're set to begin entering results. PingPong will automatically rank your teams as matches progress. Later, players in your league can make an account and join teams if they wish. This will let them track how their performance is doing across multiple teams.

FAQ

Q: What is elo?

A: Elo is a common ratings system that describes skill. Each competitor has an elo rating, which goes up when they win and down when they lose. It is adjusted for the relative skill of your opponent, so if you beat someone with a much higher rating, your rating will go up a lot, and if you lose to someone with a much lower rating (Oh Dear!) your rating will go down. A lot. Check out the Wikipedia entry on elo for more information.
